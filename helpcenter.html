Help Center

Welcome to the Native Roots Nursery Help Center! Here you can find answers to frequently asked questions, troubleshooting tips, and advice on selecting and caring for your plants.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which plants are native to my area?
You can use our Virtual Gardener feature or consult our geo-adaptive plant recommendations to find plants that are native to your specific location.
What are the benefits of planting native plants?
Native plants are better adapted to local conditions and require less water and maintenance. They also support local ecosystems and wildlife.
How can I manage pests without harming my plants?
Using natural pest control methods and selecting disease-resistant plant varieties can help manage pests effectively. Our Virtual Gardener can provide specific advice based on your plant selections.
Can I return plants if they do not thrive?
We offer a return policy for plants that fail to thrive within a month of purchase, provided care guidelines are followed. Please refer to our return policy for more details.
